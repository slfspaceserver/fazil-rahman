A Legacy of Leadership in Luxury

As the visionary Director of Classic Group of Companies and the dynamic CEO of Classic Jewels & Diamonds, Fazil Rahman has meticulously crafted a career defined by integrity, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. His leadership extends across the global gold, diamond, bullion, and real estate sectors.

With a profound understanding of market dynamics and a passion for making premium quality accessible, Fazil has successfully steered his ventures to new heights, establishing a benchmark for trust and sophistication in the luxury market.
